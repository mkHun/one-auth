Zero-trust software development

At every stage of the software development lifecycle it’s important to keep tight control over which of your team members have access to what resources. Unnecessarily wide access leaves the door open for misuse. Worse, in the event of a breach, the scope for malicious activity becomes much greater.

One Auth moves you forward on the path to zero-trust security with role-based access control (RBAC) to provision access for your architects, engineers, testers, and infrastructure specialists. Reinforcing this least privilege access approach are One Auth’s multi-factor authentication and access restriction products for a robust security posture at your tech firm.

Drive enhanced security inside and out with One Auth’s User Lifecycle Manager, AMFA and Access Manager.

Faster, smarter internal operations

As technology companies, we love our applications across every function - collaboration and project management, coding and design, testing and automation, deployment and security. But with the sheer number of applications to manage, assigning permissions to large numbers of team members is a drain on administrators. And logging in to each of these applications individually is time-consuming for users.

One Auth simplifies access for your users with its single sign-on, bringing every application within a click’s reach. This means time saved without the need for multiple logins, and the elimination of multiple sets of credentials. For admins, One Auth goes even further. Automation of provisioning and role-based access permissions reinforces security while delivering major gains in productivity.

Drive enhanced productivity in access and administration to streamline development and delivery with One Auth’s User Lifecycle Manager and SSO & IdP.

Streamlined collaboration for an enhanced customer experience

An important part of the software development process is close collaboration between clients and development teams. Seamless communication is not as straightforward as it seems, though, and poorly managed access control is often the cause of friction.

One Auth’s single sign-on makes access effortless for your customers. Viewing backlog lists, attending scrum calls, choosing from UI options, or reviewing beta versions of applications are all a click away. And smoother digital interactions translate to higher overall satisfaction.

Deliver great experiences and drive satisfaction and loyalty with One Auth’s SSO & IdP.
